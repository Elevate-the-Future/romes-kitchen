Hello, My name is Rome Rogers, owner of Romes Kitchen based out of the San Francisco Bay Area. Rome's Kitchen is an Italian soulfood restaurant I decided to create in the middle of 2019. I would work long hours at other companies therefore I decided to invest in myself and put those hours into my own company. We feed people here at Rome's kitchen. We like to deliver a unique experience through our food that is one of a kind.
